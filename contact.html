Frequently Asked Questions

What inspired your passion for music?

My passion for music began in childhood, influenced by family members who were musicians. Over time, I discovered how music could express emotions that words couldn't capture, and that realization sparked a lifelong journey of musical exploration.

How do you discover new music?

I use a combination of streaming service recommendations, music blogs, friend suggestions, and exploring related artists. I also make a point to attend live shows and festivals where I can experience new sounds firsthand.

Can I suggest music for you to listen to?

Absolutely! I'm always open to music recommendations. Please use the contact form to share your suggestions, and I'll do my best to listen and respond with my thoughts.
