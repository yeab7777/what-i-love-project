Natural Tourist Places

Discover the breathtaking beauty of Ethiopia through the collection of natural wonders.

Sof Omar Cave

The fantastic limestone caves of Sof Omar make a day's outing from Dinano, Robe or Goba. The road leaves Robe town, crossing the farming areas to the east, before descending into the lowlands.

Danakil Depression

Danakil Depression is one of the remotest spot in the world and the lowest part in unique land formation of the world Great Rift Valley system, still this part of the earth unstable and pulling each other to the opposite side.

Simien Mountains

The Simien Mountains are one of Ethiopia's most popular destinations. With its diverse flora and fauna, immense views and extraordinary geography, it's an exceptional place that can be enjoyed by hardy trekkers and casual day visitors alike.

Bale Mountains

The Bale Mountains are a mountain range in the Oromia Region of southeast Ethiopia. They include Tullu Dimtu, the second-highest mountain in Ethiopia, and Mount Batu.
